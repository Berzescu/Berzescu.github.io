Muffins with nuts and chocolate

A variation of the muffins prepared in winter, tasty at any time of the year, this dessert will delight you guaranteed.

Ingredients

  • a cup of flour;
  • Half a packet of butter;
  • half a cup of caster sugar;
  • three eggs;
  • a cup of chopped walnut kernels;
  • a cup of milk;
  • 50 g of chocolate flakes;
  • two sachets of vanilla sugar;
  • an envelope of baking powder;
  • a pinch of salt.

    • Preparation:

    Mix flour, eggs, caster sugar and vanilla; add melted butter, milk, baking powder and salt and continue to mix; add chocolate flakes and walnut kernels; pour the dough into a baking dish and bake for 25 minutes.

    Pizza with ham and mushrooms

    Pizza is a dish originating in Naples, Italy. It is a flat bread, usually round, covered with tomato sauce and cheese, plus other optional toppings, baked. Pissa is a Latin term that appeared in the ninth century, meaning "flat bread".

    Dough ingredient:

  • 500 g flour type 650;
  • 325 ml of warm water;
  • 7 g dry yeast or 25 g fresh;
  • 1 teaspoon salt;
  • Another 20 g flour for kneading;
  • a handful of semolina (for the worktop);

    • Topping ingredient:

  • 200 g fresh mozzarella;
  • 100 g Parmesan race fin;
  • 4 slices of boiled ham (like Prague, prosciutto cotto);
  • 4 slices of raw ham (like Parma or smoked Schwarzwald type);
  • 8 medium or 4 large fresh mushrooms;
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (to sprinkle on top);
  • Fresh basil leaves.

    • Preparation:

    I measured 325 ml of warm water and transferred them to a bowl. I put dry (or fresh) yeast in it and mixed it well with a teaspoon. In a large bowl I put the 500 g of flour and a teaspoon of salt and mixed them well. I poured the yeast water over them and mixed until I got a dough.

    I washed the mushrooms and cut them into thin slices. I grated the parmesan and prepared the mozzarella at hand. I heated the oven to 250 C (the highest stage for gas ones) with the baking stone placed in it.

    I spread the dough on a baking sheet.

    I sprinkled 1/2 of Parmesan over the tomato sauce (the rest comes later). I broke the slices of ham by hand and placed them on the 4 pizzas (one slice boiled and one raw-dried on each pizza). I evenly distributed the slices of mushrooms (2 mushrooms / pizza each). Pieces of mozzarella and fresh basil leaves followed. At the end I grated the rest of the Parmesan cheese and sprinkled the pizza with a little olive oil.

    Fluffy donuts

    Ingredients

  • 500 g flour;
  • 2 yolks;
  • 350 ml milk;
  • 2 sachets of vanilla sugar;
  • a pinch of salt;
  • lemon peel;
  • 1 tablespoon old;
  • 30 g fresh yeast or 10 g dry yeast;
  • 10 g vanilla powdered sugar;
  • frying oil.

    • Preparation:

    Mix the yeast with a spoonful of sugar and warm milk and leave it for a while, until it foams. Then beat well with the yolks and 300 g of flour with a mixer. Add salt, vanilla sugar, lemon peel (which do not save) and the rest of the flour. Knead to be an elastic and soft dough. Leave to rise for 30 minutes.

    After leavening, the fluffy donut dough is spread on a thin-finger-thick floured countertop. Cut the rounds with a special shape or a glass with thin edges. Mine were approx. 6 cm in diameter.

    The dough for fluffy donuts is left to rise on the tray for 10 minutes.

    Heat the oil well and put the donuts in the hot oil and turn the heat to low. Turn with 2 forks on the other side and when they turn golden, take them out on absorbent napkins, shake lightly and put them in a bowl and sprinkle with vanilla powdered sugar while they are still hot.
